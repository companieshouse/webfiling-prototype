Filing for: ACME COMPANY LIMITED (01234567)

Were any of the company’s shares admitted to trading on a market at any time during the confirmation period?

What does this question mean?
This question is asking if the company has shares admitted to one or more of the following:

  • a market established under the rules of a UK recognised investment exchange
  • any other UK regulated market or EU regulated market
  • any other market outside of the UK

A company that trades on a market is called a 'traded company'. This is different from a 'trading company', which means a company that is not dormant.
